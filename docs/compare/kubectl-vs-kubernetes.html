How to pronounce kubectl vs Kubernetes

Kubernetes is settled ("koo-ber-NET-eez"). kubectl is the long-running K8s joke — Kelsey Hightower and many maintainers say "koob-control", the community meme is "cube-cuddle", and plenty of people just spell it out. We document every reading.

kubectl /ˈkuːb kənˌtroʊl/ — koob control
Kubernetes /ˌkuːbərˈnɛtiːz/ — koober netteez
