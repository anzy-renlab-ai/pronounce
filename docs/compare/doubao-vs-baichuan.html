How to pronounce Doubao vs Baichuan

Two of China's most-cited AI labs. Doubao (豆包, ByteDance) is "doh-BOW" — the BOW as in bow-and-arrow. Baichuan (百川) is "BYE-chwahn." Both follow standard Mandarin Pinyin, but English speakers tend to spell them out letter by letter instead.

doubao /doʊ ˈbaʊ/ — doh BOW
baichuan /baɪ ˈtʃwɑːn/ — BYE chwahn
