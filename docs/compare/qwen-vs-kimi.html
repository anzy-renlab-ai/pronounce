How to pronounce Qwen vs Kimi

Two of China's top consumer chatbots. Qwen (Alibaba, 通义千问) is a single syllable "KWEN" — rhymes with "when." Kimi (Moonshot) is two syllables "KEE-mee." Both are designed to read easily in English, but Qwen routinely gets spelled out as Q-W-E-N.
