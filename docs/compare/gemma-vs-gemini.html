How to pronounce Gemma vs Gemini

Both come from Google DeepMind. Gemma ("JEM-uh") is the open-weights small model family. Gemini ("JEM-uh-nye") is the flagship multimodal chatbot. Same Latin/zodiac root, but distinct readings — Gemma ends in a soft schwa, Gemini ends in a long -nye.

gemma /ˈdʒɛ mə/ — JEM uh
Gemini /ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ/ — jem in eye
