hailuo 怎么读

产品 社区共识

high lwaw /ˈhaɪˌlwɔ/ mp3

hailuo 在英语中的标准读法是 「high lwaw」（/ˈhaɪˌlwɔ/）。

Hailuo AI is MiniMax's text/image-to-video generator (Hailuo 2.3, Media Agent; 2025). Name romanizes Chinese 海螺 hǎiluó ("conch / sea snail"), hence the English nickname "Conch Video." STRESS: HIGH-lwaw. Two syllables: "hai"="high," "luo"=pinyin [lwɔ] — a single glide-syllable like "law" with a w-onset, NOT three beats "loo-oh." Common anglicized form is "HIGH-low" (/haɪloʊ/), shown as the alt. Mandarin tones hǎi (3rd) + luó (2nd); English speakers can ignore tone.

其他读法：

  • 「high low」 /ˈhaɪˌloʊ/

来源：Wiktionary — 海螺 (hǎiluó), Mandarin IPA /xaɪ̯ lu̯ɔ/
