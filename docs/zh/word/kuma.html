Kuma 怎么读

项目 社区共识

KOO mah /ˈkuːmə/ mp3

Kuma 在英语中的标准读法是 「KOO mah」（/ˈkuːmə/）。

Envoy-based service mesh originally created and donated by Kong, accepted into the CNCF (now a Sandbox project; repo kumahq/kuma, branded with the bear emoji 🐻). The name is the Japanese word for 'bear' (くま / 熊), so the intended pronunciation follows Japanese: KOO-mah — stress on the FIRST syllable, 'oo' as in 'boot'. English speakers who don't know the origin reach for KYOO-mah (rhyming with 'puma'), which is the common mispronunciation. Standard Japanese pronunciation of くま is KOO-mah.

其他读法：

  • 「KYOO mah」 /ˈkjuːmə/

来源：Introducing Kuma, the Service Mesh From Kong (Cloud Native Now)
