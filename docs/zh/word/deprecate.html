deprecate 怎么读

计算机术语 社区共识

dep ruh kayt /ˈdɛprəkeɪt/ mp3

deprecate 在英语中的标准读法是 「dep ruh kayt」（/ˈdɛprəkeɪt/）。

"DEP-ruh-kayt" — three syllables, no "-shee-". Often mixed up with "depreciate" (deh-PREE-shee-ayt), which is a different word.

来源：Merriam-Webster
