Kuaishou 怎么读

产品 社区共识

kwai show /ˈkwaɪʃoʊ/ mp3

Kuaishou 在英语中的标准读法是 「kwai show」（/ˈkwaɪʃoʊ/）。

Kuaishou (快手, pinyin kuàishǒu, "quick hand") is the Chinese short-video company behind the Kling video model and the Kwai-Keye / KwaiVGI open models on Hugging Face. "Kuai" is one syllable that rhymes with "why" — which is exactly why the company's overseas brand is spelled Kwai — and "shou" rhymes with "show". So it is KWAI-show, not "koo-eye-shoo" or "KWAY-shoo".

来源：Wiktionary — 快手 (pinyin kuàishǒu, Sinological IPA /kʰu̯aɪ̯⁵¹ ʂoʊ̯²¹⁴/)
