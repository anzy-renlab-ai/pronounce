melange 怎么读

命令行工具 社区共识

may lahnzh /meɪˈlɑːnʒ/ mp3

melange 在英语中的标准读法是 「may lahnzh」（/meɪˈlɑːnʒ/）。

melange is Chainguard's declarative apk build tool; paired with apko it builds Wolfi-based low-CVE container images without a Dockerfile. The name is the French word mélange ("mixture"), so the final -ange is a soft "zh" like rouge or measure, and the stress falls on the second syllable: may-LAHNZH, not "MEL-anj". The anglicized may-LAHNJ is a listed dictionary variant, but the zh-form is the standard one.

其他读法：

  • 「muh lahnzh」 /məˈlɑːnʒ/
  • 「may lahnj」 /meɪˈlɑːndʒ/

来源：Wiktionary — melange: /məˈlɑnʒ/, /meɪ-/, /-ˈlɑndʒ/ (corroborated by Dictionary.com: [mey-lahnzh, -lahnj])
