MinIO 怎么读

产品 创作者已明确

min eye oh /mɪnˈaɪoʊ/ mp3

MinIO 在英语中的标准读法是 「min eye oh」（/mɪnˈaɪoʊ/）。

MinIO is a high-performance, S3-compatible object-storage server; the name is short for "minimal I/O," so it is said min-EYE-oh — the "IO" is the spelled-out input/output acronym ("eye-oh"). Founder/CEO A.B. (Anand Babu) Periasamy has explicitly stated he wants it known as "Min-IO, as in minimal IO" (TechTarget, verified). The article confirms the common variant: "most people pronounce it MIN-EO" (min-ee-oh, captured as the alt) — devs default to it because "-io" reads like an ordinary word ending rather than a spelled-out acronym.

其他读法：

  • 「min ee oh」 /ˈmɪnioʊ/

来源：TechTarget Storage Soup — founder A.B. Periasamy on the MinIO name
