E D twenty-five five one nine /ˌiː diː ˌtwɛnti faɪv faɪv ˌwʌn ˈnaɪn/ mp3

Ed25519 在英语中的标准读法是 「E D twenty-five five one nine」（/ˌiː diː ˌtwɛnti faɪv faɪv ˌwʌn ˈnaɪn/）。

"E-D-twenty-five-five-one-nine". EdDSA over Curve25519.

来源：ed25519.cr.yp.to
