AGNTCY 怎么读

项目 社区共识

ay jun see /ˈeɪdʒənsi/ mp3

AGNTCY 在英语中的标准读法是 「ay jun see」（/ˈeɪdʒənsi/）。

AGNTCY is the Linux Foundation-hosted "Internet of Agents" project donated by Cisco (Outshift), covering agent discovery (OASF), messaging (SLIM) and identity, and is routinely compared against MCP and A2A. It is a disemvoweled brand — AGNT (agent) plus -CY — and is simply read as the English word "agency" (AY-jun-see), not spelled out letter-by-letter. Cold readers usually try "A-G-N-T-C-Y" or "ag-EN-tik-ee"; the project's own site and the Linux Foundation press release never state the pronunciation, so this is labeled community-consensus on the strength of third-party technical coverage.

来源：Aisera — A2A protocol explainer: AGNTCY (pronounced "agency")
