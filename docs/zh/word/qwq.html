QwQ 怎么读

产品 创作者已明确

kwyoo /kwjuː/ mp3

QwQ 在英语中的标准读法是 「kwyoo」（/kwjuː/）。

Alibaba's Qwen-team reasoning model (QwQ = 'Qwen with Questions'). The official Qwen blog states verbatim: 'Note: This is the pronunciation of QwQ: /kwjuː/, similar to the word "quill".' So it's a single syllable: a 'kw' onset glued onto the sound of the letter Q (/juː/), i.e. KWYOO — NOT spelled out 'Q-W-Q' and NOT the kaomoji 'qwq' face. The creators' 'similar to quill' is an informal onset hint (shared 'kw-'), not a literal rhyme — /kwjuː/ does not end in an L and does not match quill's short 'i'. Lowercase middle 'w' makes it look like an emoticon, which is exactly why almost nobody guesses the intended sound.

其他读法：

  • 「quill」

来源：Official Qwen team blog (Alibaba Cloud), QwQ-32B-Preview release post
