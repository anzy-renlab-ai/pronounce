Pronunciation of the day: tongyi

tongyi is pronounced "tohng yee"  ·  /ˈtʊŋˈji/  ·  product

▶ tohng yee mp3

Alibaba's Tongyi (通义) brand umbrella over Qwen / Tongyi Qianwen (通义千问, pinyin Tōngyì Qiānwèn), surfaced across Alibaba Cloud Model Studio and Hugging Face. Pinyin Tōngyì: tōng (通, rounded vowel of English "song") + yì (义, "yee"). STRESS-pattern: TOHNG-yee, roughly even with a slight lift on the first syllable. English speakers often mangle it as "tongue-ee" or "tahn-jee"; the o is the rounded "ong" of "song," not "tongue."

For the full source citation, alternate readings, and CLI usage, see the canonical page → tongyi.
