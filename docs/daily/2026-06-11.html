Pronunciation of the day: Pydantic

Pydantic is pronounced "pie dantick"  ·  /paɪˈdæntɪk/  ·  product

▶ pie dantick mp3

"pie-DAN-tic" — "py" pronounced "pie" (as in Python), then "dantic". Heard from Samuel Colvin (creator) in Talk Python To Me / FastAPI talks; no official FAQ entry on docs.pydantic.dev as of this writing.
