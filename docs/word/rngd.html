How to pronounce RNGD

product creator-clarified

ren uh gayd /ˈrɛnɪˌɡeɪd/ mp3

RNGD is most commonly pronounced "ren uh gayd" (/ˈrɛnɪˌɡeɪd/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see FuriosaAI — RNGD product page ("RNGD (pronounced \"renegade\")")).

RNGD is FuriosaAI's second-generation data-center inference NPU (48GB HBM3, 180W, tensor-contraction architecture), pitched as a power-efficient Nvidia alternative and in mass production since January 2026. The vowel-less name is a disemvoweled spelling of "renegade" — Furiosa's own product page and its Hot Chips 2024 press release both gloss it as RNGD (pronounced "renegade"), and the company brands its spec page and user summit as RENEGADE. Reading it letter-by-letter as R-N-G-D or guessing "ringed" is the common mistake.

Source: FuriosaAI — RNGD product page ("RNGD (pronounced \"renegade\")")
