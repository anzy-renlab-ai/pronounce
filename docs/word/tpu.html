How to pronounce TPU

acronym community-consensus

T P U /ˌtiː piː ˈjuː/ mp3

TPU is most commonly pronounced "T P U" (/ˌtiː piː ˈjuː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Letters "T-P-U". Tensor Processing Unit.

Source: Google Cloud
