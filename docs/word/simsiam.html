SimSiam is most commonly pronounced "sim sigh am" (/sɪmˈsaɪæm/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Negative-free Siamese SSL that avoids collapse via a stop-gradient; portmanteau of 'Simple Siamese', so the tail is read like 'Siam' — 'sim-SY-am'.

Source: Chen & He, 'Exploring Simple Siamese Representation Learning', FAIR (arXiv 2011.10566)

How do you pronounce SimSiam?
