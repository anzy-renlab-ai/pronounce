How to pronounce idempotent

cs-term contested

eye dem po tent /ˌaɪdɛmˈpoʊtənt/ mp3
or: id em po tent /ˌɪdɛmˈpoʊtənt/

idempotent is most commonly pronounced "eye dem po tent" (/ˌaɪdɛmˈpoʊtənt/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"eye-dem-PO-tent" vs "id-em-PO-tent".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "id em po tent" /ˌɪdɛmˈpoʊtənt/

Source: Wikipedia
