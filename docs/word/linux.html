How to pronounce Linux

product community-consensus

linnix /ˈlɪnəks/ mp3

Linux is most commonly pronounced "linnix" (/ˈlɪnəks/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Linus's own pronunciation is "LIN-ux" (short I, schwa). Wikipedia documents the variants.

Source: Wikipedia § Pronunciation
