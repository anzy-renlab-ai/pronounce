How to pronounce ECMA

acronym contested

EK ma /ˈɛk mə/ mp3
or: E C M A /ˌiː siː ɛm ˈeɪ/

ECMA is most commonly pronounced "EK ma" (/ˈɛk mə/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"EK-ma" (one word) is the org's own usage; letter-by-letter also common. European Computer Manufacturers Association (legacy name).

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "E C M A" /ˌiː siː ɛm ˈeɪ/

Source: Ecma International

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
