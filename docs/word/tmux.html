How to pronounce tmux

tool contested

T mux /ˌtiː ˈmʌks/ mp3
or: tee mucks /ˈtiːmʌks/

tmux is most commonly pronounced "T mux" (/ˌtiː ˈmʌks/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"T-mux" or "TEE-mucks".

How do you pronounce tmux?

What is the IPA for tmux?
