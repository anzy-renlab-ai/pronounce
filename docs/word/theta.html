How to pronounce theta

cs-term contested

THAY tuh /ˈθeɪ tə/ mp3
or: THEE tuh /ˈθiː tə/

theta is most commonly pronounced "THAY tuh" (/ˈθeɪ tə/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.
