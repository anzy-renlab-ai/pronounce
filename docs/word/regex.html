How to pronounce regex

cs-term contested

rej eks /ˈrɛdʒɛks/ mp3
or: reg eks /ˈrɛɡɛks/

regex is most commonly pronounced "rej eks" (/ˈrɛdʒɛks/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

How do you pronounce regex?

What is the IPA for regex?

The IPA for regex is /ˈrɛdʒɛks/, respelled "rej eks".

Is regex sometimes pronounced "reg eks"?

Yes — "reg eks" (/ˈrɛɡɛks/) is one of the alternate readings of regex in active use.
