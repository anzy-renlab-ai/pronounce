How to pronounce Lovelace

product community-consensus

LUV lace /ˈlʌv leɪs/ mp3

Lovelace is most commonly pronounced "LUV lace" (/ˈlʌv leɪs/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"LUV-lace" (RTX 40-series), after Ada Lovelace.

Source: NVIDIA Ada Lovelace
