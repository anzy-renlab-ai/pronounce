How to pronounce Janus

product community-consensus

JAY nuhs /ˈdʒeɪnəs/ mp3

Janus is most commonly pronounced "JAY nuhs" (/ˈdʒeɪnəs/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

DeepSeek's unified multimodal model (Janus / Janus-Pro) that decouples visual encoding for understanding vs generation. Named after the two-faced Roman god of doorways: JAY-nuhs, stress on the first syllable.

Source: DeepSeek — Janus paper (arXiv 2410.13848)
