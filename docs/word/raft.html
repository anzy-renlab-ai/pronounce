How to pronounce Raft

cs-term creator-clarified

raft /ræft/ mp3

Raft is most commonly pronounced "raft" (/ræft/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see raft.github.io).

"raft" the word. Consensus algorithm by Diego Ongaro + John Ousterhout, intended as "more understandable than Paxos".

Source: raft.github.io

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
