How to pronounce Fugatto

product community-consensus

foo gah toh /fuˈɡɑːtoʊ/ mp3

Fugatto is most commonly pronounced "foo gah toh" (/fuˈɡɑːtoʊ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Fugatto = "Foundational Generative Audio Transformer Opus 1," NVIDIA's Nov-2024 any-to-any generative audio model (text/audio prompts to create or transform music, voice, and sound). Pronounced foo-GAH-toh, stress on the second syllable (GAH = long "ah" as in father), echoing the Italian musical term "fugato" (in the manner of a fugue) — NVIDIA doubled the t for the backronym (Italian speakers note the real word is one-t "fugato"). NVIDIA never published an explicit pronunciation, so this reading is inferred from the fugato etymon (community-consensus, not creator-clarified). Non-native devs commonly flatten it to FOO-gat-oh or few-GAT-oh, misplacing stress and using a short American "a."

Source: NVIDIA Research — Fugatto 1: Foundational Generative Audio Transformer Opus 1
