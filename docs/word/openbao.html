How do you pronounce OpenBao?

OpenBao is pronounced "open bao" (/ˌoʊpən ˈbaʊ/). Linux Foundation fork of HashiCorp Vault named after baozi, the steamed bun (per GitHub discussion #34) — "bao" rhymes with "cow", not "bay-oh". Source: Wiktionary — bao.
