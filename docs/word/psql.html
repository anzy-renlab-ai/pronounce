How to pronounce psql

cli-tool community-consensus

pee ess cue ell /ˌpiː ɛs kjuː ˈɛl/ mp3

psql is most commonly pronounced "pee ess cue ell" (/ˌpiː ɛs kjuː ˈɛl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Postgres CLI; usually letters "P-S-Q-L".

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
