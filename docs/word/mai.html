How to pronounce MAI

acronym community-consensus

M A I /ˌɛm eɪ ˈaɪ/ mp3

MAI is most commonly pronounced "M A I" (/ˌɛm eɪ ˈaɪ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Microsoft AI's in-house model family (MAI-Thinking-1, MAI-Voice-2, ...); read letter-by-letter like the "AI" it contains — Microsoft has published no official pronunciation.

Source: Microsoft AI — seven new MAI models (Build 2026)
