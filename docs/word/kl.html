How to pronounce KL

abbreviation community-consensus

kay ell /ˌkeɪ ˈɛl/ mp3

KL is most commonly pronounced "kay ell" (/ˌkeɪ ˈɛl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Kullback-Leibler (divergence); letters "K-L".

Source: Wikipedia
