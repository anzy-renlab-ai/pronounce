How to pronounce chezmoi

cli-tool creator-clarified

shay mwa /ʃeɪ mwa/ mp3

chezmoi is most commonly pronounced "shay mwa" (/ʃeɪ mwa/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see chezmoi official FAQ (Design)).

CLI dotfiles manager. Name = French "chez moi" ("at my house"). Official FAQ documents the pronunciation as /ʃeɪ mwa/ "shay-mwa". Non-obvious because the spelling looks English/German; the "z" is silent and "oi" is the French [wa], not English "oy".
