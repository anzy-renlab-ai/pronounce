How to pronounce URL

acronym contested

U R L /ˌjuː ɑːr ˈɛl/ mp3
or: earl /ɜːrl/

URL is most commonly pronounced "U R L" (/ˌjuː ɑːr ˈɛl/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Letter-by-letter dominant; Tim Berners-Lee uses "earl" but it never stuck.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "earl" /ɜːrl/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
