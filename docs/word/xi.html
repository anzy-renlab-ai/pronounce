How do you pronounce xi?

What is the IPA for xi?

The IPA for xi is /zaɪ/, respelled "zye".

Is xi sometimes pronounced "k sigh"?

Yes — "k sigh" (/ksaɪ/) is one of the alternate readings of xi in active use.
