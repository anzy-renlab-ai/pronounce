How do you pronounce Debian?

Debian is pronounced "deb ee un" (/ˈdɛbiən/). "DEB-ee-uhn" — portmanteau of Debra + Ian Murdock, documented on the about page. Source: Debian about.

What is the IPA for Debian?

The IPA for Debian is /ˈdɛbiən/, respelled "deb ee un".
