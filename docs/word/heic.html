How to pronounce heic

abbreviation contested

aitch ee eye see /ˌeɪtʃ iː aɪ ˈsiː/ mp3
or: hake /heɪk/

heic is most commonly pronounced "aitch ee eye see" (/ˌeɪtʃ iː aɪ ˈsiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Apple High-Efficiency image; spelled "H-E-I-C", some say "hake".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "hake" /heɪk/
