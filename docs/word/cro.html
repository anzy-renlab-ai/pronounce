How to pronounce CRO

acronym community-consensus

see ar oh /ˌsiː ɑːr ˈoʊ/ mp3

CRO is most commonly pronounced "see ar oh" (/ˌsiː ɑːr ˈoʊ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Chief Revenue Officer; letters "C-R-O".

Source: Wikipedia
