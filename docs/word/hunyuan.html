How do you pronounce Hunyuan?

Hunyuan is pronounced "hoon yoo en" (/ˌhuːn juˈɑːn/). Tencent 混元 "hùn yuán"; approximated "HOON-yoo-en". Source: Tencent Hunyuan.

What is the IPA for Hunyuan?

The IPA for Hunyuan is /ˌhuːn juˈɑːn/, respelled "hoon yoo en".
