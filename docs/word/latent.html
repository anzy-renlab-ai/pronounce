How to pronounce latent

cs-term community-consensus

lay tunt /ˈleɪtənt/ mp3

latent is most commonly pronounced "lay tunt" (/ˈleɪtənt/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Hidden (latent space, latent diffusion); "LAY-tunt".
