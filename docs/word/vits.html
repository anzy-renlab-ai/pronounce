How to pronounce vits

product contested

vits /vɪts/ mp3
or: vee eye tee ess /ˌviː aɪ tiː ˈɛs/

vits is most commonly pronounced "vits" (/vɪts/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

End-to-end TTS model; "vits" or spelled out.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "vee eye tee ess" /ˌviː aɪ tiː ˈɛs/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
