How to pronounce bayesian

cs-term contested

bay zee un /ˈbeɪziən/ mp3
or: bay zhun /ˈbeɪʒən/

bayesian is most commonly pronounced "bay zee un" (/ˈbeɪziən/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

From Bayes; "BAY-zee-un" or "BAY-zhun".
