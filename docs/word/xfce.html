How to pronounce xfce

product community-consensus

eks eff see ee /ˌɛks ɛf siː ˈiː/ mp3

xfce is most commonly pronounced "eks eff see ee" (/ˌɛks ɛf siː ˈiː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Lightweight desktop environment; letters "X-F-C-E".

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
