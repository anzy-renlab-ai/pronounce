How to pronounce Hymba

cs-term contested

HIM buh /ˈhɪm.bə/ mp3
or: HYME buh /ˈhaɪm.bə/

Hymba is most commonly pronounced "HIM buh" (/ˈhɪm.bə/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

NVIDIA's 1.5B hybrid-head small language model (Nov 2024) that runs Mamba (state-space) and attention heads in parallel within the same layer. The name is widely read as a blend of 'HYbrid' + 'Mamba' (secondary write-ups confirm this; NVIDIA's own blog never states the etymology, so the portmanteau is community inference). That blend implies a short 'y' — HIM-buh, a homophone of the Namibian Himba people — and stress falls on the first syllable. But the 'Hy-' spelling leads many English speakers to default to HYME-buh (long i, like 'hybrid'), so usage is split. No official NVIDIA pronunciation exists.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "HYME buh" /ˈhaɪm.bə/

Source: NVIDIA Technical Blog — Hymba
