How to pronounce manus

product community-consensus

MAH nuhs /ˈmɑː nəs/ mp3

manus is most commonly pronounced "MAH nuhs" (/ˈmɑː nəs/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

General-purpose AI agent. Latin for "hand" — "MAH-nuhs."
