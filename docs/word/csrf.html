How to pronounce CSRF

acronym contested

sea surf /ˈsiː sɜːrf/ mp3
or: C S R F /ˌsiː ɛs ɑːr ˈɛf/

CSRF is most commonly pronounced "sea surf" (/ˈsiː sɜːrf/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

OWASP-favored "sea-surf"; letter-by-letter common too.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "C S R F" /ˌsiː ɛs ɑːr ˈɛf/

Source: OWASP

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
