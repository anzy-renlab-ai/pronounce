How to pronounce Weights & Biases

product community-consensus

weights and biases /weɪts ænd ˈbaɪəsɪz/ mp3

Weights & Biases is most commonly pronounced "weights and biases" (/weɪts ænd ˈbaɪəsɪz/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"weights-and-BYE-uh-siz".
