How to pronounce CISO

acronym contested

see soh /ˈsiːsoʊ/ mp3
or: see eye ess oh /ˌsiː aɪ ɛs ˈoʊ/

CISO is most commonly pronounced "see soh" (/ˈsiːsoʊ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Chief Information Security Officer; "SEE-soh" is dominant, letters also heard.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "see eye ess oh" /ˌsiː aɪ ɛs ˈoʊ/

Source: Wikipedia

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
