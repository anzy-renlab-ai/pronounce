cs-term community-consensus

uh blay shun /əˈbleɪʃən/ mp3

ablation is most commonly pronounced "uh blay shun" (/əˈbleɪʃən/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Ablation study: remove a part to measure its effect; "uh-BLAY-shun".
