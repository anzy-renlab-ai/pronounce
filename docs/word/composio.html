How to pronounce composio

project community-consensus

kum POE zee oh /kəm ˈpoʊ zi oʊ/ mp3

composio is most commonly pronounced "kum POE zee oh" (/kəm ˈpoʊ zi oʊ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Tool-use platform for AI agents. "kum-POE-zee-oh."

Source: Composio

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
