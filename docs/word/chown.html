How to pronounce chown

cli-tool contested

choun /tʃaʊn/ mp3
or: ch own /ˌtʃ ˈoʊn/

chown is most commonly pronounced "choun" (/tʃaʊn/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"change owner"; rhymes-with-"clown" or spelled "CH-own".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ch own" /ˌtʃ ˈoʊn/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
