How to pronounce Btrfs

product contested

BUTTER F S /ˈbʌt ər ɛf ɛs/ mp3
or: B T R F S /ˌbiː tiː ɑːr ɛf ˈɛs/

Btrfs is most commonly pronounced "BUTTER F S" (/ˈbʌt ər ɛf ɛs/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Chris Mason / kernel folks: "butter-F-S" (B-Tree File System). Letter-by-letter also common.
