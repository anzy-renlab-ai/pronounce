How to pronounce Liger

product community-consensus

lie gher /ˈlaɪɡər/ mp3

Liger is most commonly pronounced "lie gher" (/ˈlaɪɡər/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

LinkedIn's Liger-Kernel is a Triton kernel library for LLM training, giving roughly 20% more throughput and 60% less memory, and it is wired into TRL, Axolotl, LLaMA-Factory and the Hugging Face Trainer. The name is the lion-tiger hybrid, so it is "LY-ger" with a long i and a hard g — not "LEE-ger", not short-i "LIG-er", and not soft-g "LY-jer". LinkedIn also backronyms it as GPU Efficient Runtime, but it is always spoken as the animal word.

Source: Wiktionary — liger, GenAm /ˈlaɪɡɚ/, blend of lion + tiger
