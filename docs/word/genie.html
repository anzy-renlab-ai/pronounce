How to pronounce Genie

product community-consensus

Genie is most commonly pronounced "JEE nee" (/ˈdʒiːni/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

DeepMind's foundation world model (Genie 1/2/3) that turns a single image or prompt into a playable, interactive environment. Pronounced like the lamp genie: JEE-nee.

Source: Genie paper (DeepMind, arXiv 2402.15391)
