How to pronounce xai

project community-consensus

X A I /ˌɛks eɪ ˈaɪ/ mp3

xai is most commonly pronounced "X A I" (/ˌɛks eɪ ˈaɪ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Elon Musk's AI company. Spell out the letters: "X-A-I." Not "zai."

Source: xAI official

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
