How to pronounce LGTM

acronym community-consensus

L G T M /ˌɛl dʒiː tiː ˈɛm/ mp3

LGTM is most commonly pronounced "L G T M" (/ˌɛl dʒiː tiː ˈɛm/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"L-G-T-M" — "looks good to me".

Source: dev community

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
