How do you pronounce phi?

phi is pronounced "fai" (/faɪ/). Microsoft's small-model series. Greek letter φ — US "FAI"; British/math "FEE." Source: Microsoft Phi.

What is the IPA for phi?

The IPA for phi is /faɪ/, respelled "fai".

Is phi sometimes pronounced "fee"?

Yes — "fee" (/fiː/) is one of the alternate readings of phi in active use.
