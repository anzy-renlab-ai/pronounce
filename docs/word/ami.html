How to pronounce AMI

acronym contested

A M I /ˌeɪ ɛm ˈaɪ/ mp3
or: ah mee /ɑːˈmiː/

AMI is most commonly pronounced "A M I" (/ˌeɪ ɛm ˈaɪ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Two names share the letters: AWS’s Amazon Machine Image is read letter-by-letter A-M-I (with "ah-mee" a common informal reading), while Yann LeCun’s AMI Labs is creator-clarified as "ah-MEE" — French for "friend".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ah mee" /ɑːˈmiː/

Source: MIT Technology Review — Yann LeCun’s AMI Labs ("ami", French for friend)
