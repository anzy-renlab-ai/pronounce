How to pronounce Byzantine

cs-term contested

biz AN teen /bɪˈzæn tiːn/ mp3
or: BIZ un tine /ˈbɪz ən taɪn/

Byzantine is most commonly pronounced "biz AN teen" (/bɪˈzæn tiːn/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"biz-AN-teen" US default; "BIZ-un-tine" UK / older usage. Byzantine fault tolerance.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "BIZ un tine" /ˈbɪz ən taɪn/

Source: Lamport 1982
