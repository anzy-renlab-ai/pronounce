How to pronounce tokei

cli-tool community-consensus

toh KAY /toʊˈkeɪ/ mp3

tokei is most commonly pronounced "toh KAY" (/toʊˈkeɪ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"toh-KAY". Japanese 時計 (clock). Counts lines of code.

Source: XAMPPRocky/tokei
