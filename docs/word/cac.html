How to pronounce CAC

acronym contested

C A C /ˌsiː eɪ ˈsiː/ mp3
or: cack /kæk/

CAC is most commonly pronounced "C A C" (/ˌsiː eɪ ˈsiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"C-A-C" letter-by-letter dominant in tech; "cack" (rhymes with back) sometimes heard. Customer Acquisition Cost.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "cack" /kæk/
