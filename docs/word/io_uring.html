How to pronounce io_uring

cs-term creator-clarified

I O YOUR ing /ˌaɪ oʊ ˈjʊər ɪŋ/ mp3
or: I O OOR ing /ˌaɪ oʊ ˈʊər ɪŋ/

io_uring is most commonly pronounced "I O YOUR ing" (/ˌaɪ oʊ ˈjʊər ɪŋ/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see Jens Axboe paper).

Jens Axboe: "I-O ring" (the underscore is silent). Async I/O syscall API.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "I O OOR ing" /ˌaɪ oʊ ˈʊər ɪŋ/

Source: Jens Axboe paper

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will. Pronounce is a community-maintained dictionary — every entry tagged with a confidence level and (where possible) a citable source.

Hear it from the command line

$ say-it io_uring # primary × 3 + audible "or: …" for each alternate
$ say-it --alt io_uring # focus on the first alternate
$ say-it --all io_uring # primary AND every alternate, each repeated
$ say-it --solo io_uring # primary only, no "or: …" tail
$ say-it --why io_uring # print the dict entry with source URL
