How to pronounce egocentric

cs-term community-consensus

ee go sen trik /ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ mp3

egocentric is most commonly pronounced "ee go sen trik" (/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

First-person (egocentric video); "ee-go-SEN-trik".
