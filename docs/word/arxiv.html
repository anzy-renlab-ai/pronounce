How to pronounce arxiv

product creator-clarified

ar kive /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ mp3

arxiv is most commonly pronounced "ar kive" (/ˈɑːrkaɪv/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see arXiv FAQ).

"AR-kive" — the X is Greek chi (/k/), so it sounds like "archive". Not "arks-iv".

Source: arXiv FAQ
