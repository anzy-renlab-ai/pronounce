How to pronounce Ornstein-Uhlenbeck

cs-term community-consensus

orn stine oo len bek /ˈɔːrnstaɪn ˈuːlənbɛk/ mp3
or: orn steen oo len bek /ˈɔːrnstiːn ˈuːlənbɛk/

Ornstein-Uhlenbeck is most commonly pronounced "orn stine oo len bek" (/ˈɔːrnstaɪn ˈuːlənbɛk/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

After Dutch physicists Leonard Ornstein and George Uhlenbeck; 'ORN-stine OO-len-bek', Uhlenbeck not 'YOO-len-bek'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "orn steen oo len bek" /ˈɔːrnstiːn ˈuːlənbɛk/

Source: Wikipedia
