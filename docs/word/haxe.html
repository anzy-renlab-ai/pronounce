How to pronounce Haxe

Haxe is most commonly pronounced "hex" (/hɛks/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Cross-platform language compiling to JS, C++, JVM and more; early official docs said "hex" while English speakers naturally say "hacks" — no creator ruling, both survive at conferences.

How do you pronounce Haxe?

Haxe is pronounced "hex" (/hɛks/). Cross-platform language compiling to JS, C++, JVM and more; early official docs said "hex" while English speakers naturally say "hacks" — no creator ruling, both survive at conferences. Source: haxelang mailing list — "hacks", "hex" or "hacksy"?.

What is the IPA for Haxe?

The IPA for Haxe is /hɛks/, respelled "hex".

Is Haxe sometimes pronounced "hacks"?

Yes — "hacks" (/hæks/) is one of the alternate readings of Haxe in active use.
