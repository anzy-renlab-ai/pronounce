How to pronounce Barlow Twins

product community-consensus

bar loh twinz /ˈbɑːrloʊ ˈtwɪnz/ mp3

Barlow Twins is most commonly pronounced "bar loh twinz" (/ˈbɑːrloʊ ˈtwɪnz/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

SSL whose loss drives the cross-correlation matrix of two identical network 'twins' toward the identity; plain English, named for neuroscientist Horace Barlow's redundancy-reduction principle — 'BAR-loh twinz'.

Source: Zbontar, Jing, Misra, LeCun & Deny, 'Barlow Twins' (arXiv 2103.03230)
