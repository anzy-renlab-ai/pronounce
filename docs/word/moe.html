How to pronounce moe

acronym contested

M O E /ˌɛm oʊ ˈiː/ mp3
or: moh /moʊ/

moe is most commonly pronounced "M O E" (/ˌɛm oʊ ˈiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Mixture of Experts. Spelled "M-O-E" in papers; some pronounce it "MOH" like the Three Stooges character.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "moh" /moʊ/

Source: HuggingFace MoE blog

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
