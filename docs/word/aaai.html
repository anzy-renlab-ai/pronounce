How to pronounce AAAI

acronym contested

triple ay eye /ˌtrɪpəl eɪ ˈaɪ/ mp3
or: ay ay ay eye /ˌeɪ eɪ eɪ ˈaɪ/

AAAI is most commonly pronounced "triple ay eye" (/ˌtrɪpəl eɪ ˈaɪ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"Triple-A-I" common in talks; letters also heard.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ay ay ay eye" /ˌeɪ eɪ eɪ ˈaɪ/

Source: AAAI

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will. Hear it from the command line

$ say-it AAAI # primary × 3 + audible "or: …" for each alternate
$ say-it --alt AAAI # focus on the first alternate
$ say-it --all AAAI # primary AND every alternate, each repeated
$ say-it --solo AAAI # primary only, no "or: …" tail
$ say-it --why AAAI # print the dict entry with source URL
