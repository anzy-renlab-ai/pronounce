How to pronounce nvlink

product community-consensus

en vee link /ˌɛnˈviː lɪŋk/ mp3

nvlink is most commonly pronounced "en vee link" (/ˌɛnˈviː lɪŋk/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

NVIDIA's GPU-to-GPU interconnect (constant in GB200/H100 cluster talk). The "NV" prefix is the NVIDIA initialism, read as the letters N-V ("en-vee"), then plain "link": en-vee-LINK. Same NV-prefix pattern as NVSwitch. NOT "nuv-link" or "en-vid-ee-uh link" — a common non-native slip. STRESS: en-vee-LINK. No official FAQ documents the pronunciation; letter-initialism reading is universal in the community.
