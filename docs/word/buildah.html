How to pronounce Buildah

cli-tool creator-clarified

BIL duh /ˈbɪl də/ mp3

Buildah is most commonly pronounced "BIL duh" (/ˈbɪl də/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see buildah.io).

"BIL-duh" — Boston-accent "builder". Dan Walsh (creator) has confirmed in multiple talks.

Source: buildah.io
