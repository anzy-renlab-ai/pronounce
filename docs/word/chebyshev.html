How to pronounce Chebyshev

person contested

cheb ih shoff /ˈtʃɛbɪʃɔːf/ mp3
or: cheb ih shef /ˌtʃɛbɪˈʃɛf/
or: cheb ih shev /ˌtʃɛbɪˈʃɛv/

Chebyshev is most commonly pronounced "cheb ih shoff" (/ˈtʃɛbɪʃɔːf/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Russian Pafnuty Chebyshev; closest is 'CHEB-ih-shoff' (-off ending), but English math often says 'cheb-ih-SHEV'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "cheb ih shef" /ˌtʃɛbɪˈʃɛf/
  • "cheb ih shev" /ˌtʃɛbɪˈʃɛv/

Source: Wikipedia
