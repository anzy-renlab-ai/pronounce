How to pronounce Rails

product community-consensus

rails /reɪlz/ mp3

Rails is most commonly pronounced "rails" (/reɪlz/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"rails" — Ruby on Rails.

Source: Ruby on Rails

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
