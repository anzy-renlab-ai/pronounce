How to pronounce Wasserstein

person contested

vah ser shtine /ˈvɑːsərʃtaɪn/ mp3
or: wah ser stine /ˈwɒsərˌstaɪn/

Wasserstein is most commonly pronounced "vah ser shtine" (/ˈvɑːsərʃtaɪn/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Leonid Vaserštejn — German-style "VAH-ser-shtine" is closest to source; "WAH-ser-stine" common in ML talks.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "wah ser stine" /ˈwɒsərˌstaɪn/
