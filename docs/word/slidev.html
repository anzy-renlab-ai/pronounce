How to pronounce Slidev

tool creator-clarified

SLIE dev /ˈslaɪ dɛv/ mp3

Slidev is most commonly pronounced "SLIE dev" (/ˈslaɪ dɛv/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see Slidev).

Anthony Fu (creator): "SLIE-dev"; slide + dev.

Source: Slidev
