How to pronounce opengl

abbreviation community-consensus

open jee ell /ˌoʊpən dʒiː ˈɛl/ mp3

opengl is most commonly pronounced "open jee ell" (/ˌoʊpən dʒiː ˈɛl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Graphics API; "open-G-L".
