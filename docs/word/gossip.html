How to pronounce gossip

cs-term community-consensus

gossip /ˈɡɒs ɪp/ mp3

gossip is most commonly pronounced "gossip" (/ˈɡɒs ɪp/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

English word. Epidemic/anti-entropy protocol family.

Source: Wikipedia
