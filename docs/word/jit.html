How to pronounce JIT

acronym contested

jit /dʒɪt/ mp3
or: J I T /ˌdʒeɪ aɪ ˈtiː/

JIT is most commonly pronounced "jit" (/dʒɪt/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"jit" (one syllable) or "J-I-T".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "J I T" /ˌdʒeɪ aɪ ˈtiː/

Source: Wikipedia
