cs-term community-consensus

ree bayss /ˈriːbeɪs/ mp3

rebase is most commonly pronounced "ree bayss" (/ˈriːbeɪs/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

git rebase: "REE-bayss".
