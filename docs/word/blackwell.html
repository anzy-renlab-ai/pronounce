How to pronounce Blackwell

product community-consensus

BLACK well /ˈblæk wɛl/ mp3

Blackwell is most commonly pronounced "BLACK well" (/ˈblæk wɛl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"BLACK-well". NVIDIA B100/B200 architecture, named after mathematician David Blackwell.

Source: NVIDIA Blackwell

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
