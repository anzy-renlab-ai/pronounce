How to pronounce veo

product contested

vee oh /ˈviːoʊ/ mp3
or: vay oh /ˈveɪoʊ/

veo is most commonly pronounced "vee oh" (/ˈviːoʊ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Google DeepMind's text-to-video model (Veo 3 / 3.1, 2025), used inside Gemini and Flow. Dominant English reading is VEE-oh; HowToPronounce gives /ˈviːoʊ/. A VAY-oh variant exists, echoing Spanish "veo" (I see). No official creator pronunciation is documented, hence contested. STRESS: VEE-oh.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "vay oh" /ˈveɪoʊ/
