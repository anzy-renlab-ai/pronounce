How to pronounce Saga

cs-term community-consensus

SAH guh /ˈsɑː ɡə/ mp3

Saga is most commonly pronounced "SAH guh" (/ˈsɑː ɡə/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"SAH-guh". Long-running distributed transaction pattern.
