How to pronounce tavily

project community-consensus

TAV uh lee /ˈtæ və li/ mp3

tavily is most commonly pronounced "TAV uh lee" (/ˈtæ və li/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

AI-optimized search API. "TAV-uh-lee."

Source: Tavily
