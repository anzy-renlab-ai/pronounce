How to pronounce baichuan

project community-consensus

BYE chwahn /baɪ ˈtʃwɑːn/ mp3

baichuan is most commonly pronounced "BYE chwahn" (/baɪ ˈtʃwɑːn/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Chinese LLM lab 百川. Pinyin bǎi-chuān → "BYE-chwahn."
