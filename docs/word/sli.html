How to pronounce SLI

acronym community-consensus

SLI is most commonly pronounced "S L I" (/ˌɛs ɛl ˈaɪ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Letters "S-L-I". Service Level Indicator.
