How to pronounce a11y

abbreviation creator-clarified

A eleven Y /ˌeɪ ɪˈlɛvən waɪ/ mp3

a11y is most commonly pronounced "A eleven Y" (/ˌeɪ ɪˈlɛvən waɪ/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see A11y Project).

"A-eleven-Y". 11 letters between a and y in "accessibility".

Source: A11y Project

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
