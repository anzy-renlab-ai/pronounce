How to pronounce nemotron

product community-consensus

nee moh tron /ˈniːmoʊˌtrɑːn/ mp3
or: nem oh tron /ˈnɛməˌtrɑːn/

nemotron is most commonly pronounced "nee moh tron" (/ˈniːmoʊˌtrɑːn/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

NVIDIA's flagship open LLM family (Nemotron 3: Nano/Super/Ultra, 2025-2026), hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE for agentic reasoning. Coined name = NVIDIA's NeMo framework ("NEural MOdules", said NEE-mo) + the -tron suffix, so the prevailing reading is first-syllable-stressed NEE-moh-tron. Some devs mis-read it as nem-oh-TRON / nuh-MOT-ron; the NeMo etymology favors NEE-moh-tron. No official creator-documented pronunciation in text. STRESS: NEE-moh-tron.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "nem oh tron" /ˈnɛməˌtrɑːn/

Source: Wikipedia — Nemotron
