How to pronounce ROUGE

acronym creator-clarified

roozh /ruːʒ/ mp3

ROUGE is most commonly pronounced "roozh" (/ruːʒ/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see Lin 2004).

Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation; French "rouge" — "roozh".

Source: Lin 2004

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
