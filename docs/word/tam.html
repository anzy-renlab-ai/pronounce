How to pronounce TAM

acronym contested

tam /tæm/ mp3
or: T A M /ˌtiː eɪ ˈɛm/

TAM is most commonly pronounced "tam" (/tæm/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"tam" (rhymes with ham) is standard in VC/PM; some say "T-A-M". Total Addressable Market.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "T A M" /ˌtiː eɪ ˈɛm/

Source: Wikipedia

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will. Pronounce is a community-maintained dictionary — every entry tagged with a confidence level and (where possible) a citable source.

Hear it from the command line

$ say-it TAM # primary × 3 + audible "or: …" for each alternate
$ say-it --alt TAM # focus on the first alternate
$ say-it --all TAM # primary AND every alternate, each repeated
$ say-it --solo TAM # primary only, no "or: …" tail
$ say-it --why TAM # print the dict entry with source URL
