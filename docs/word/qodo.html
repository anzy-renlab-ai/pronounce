How to pronounce Qodo

product community-consensus

koh doh /ˈkoʊdoʊ/ mp3

Qodo is most commonly pronounced "koh doh" (/ˈkoʊdoʊ/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Qodo (formerly CodiumAI/Codium, rebranded 2024) is an AI code-review and test-generation agent platform. Its official rebrand blog confirms the name is a confluence of "Quality" and "Code" (and "quality of development") — but the blog does NOT state a pronunciation. Said KOH-doh: first syllable like "code," stress on syllable one. Pronunciation is community consensus (howtopronounce lists /ˈkoʊdoʊ/, "koh-doh"), consistent with the "Code" root — not an explicit creator statement. Non-native devs, thrown by a bare Q with no following U, often overcorrect to KWOH-doh (treating Q like "queen"/"quick"), but no /kw/ glide belongs here — this Q is a stylized C, not a normal English Q.

Source: Qodo — "Introducing Qodo (formerly Codium)" rebrand announcement
