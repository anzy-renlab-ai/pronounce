How to pronounce poettering

person contested

pet er ing /ˈpœtərɪŋ/ mp3
or: poh eh ter ing /ˈpoʊətərɪŋ/

poettering is most commonly pronounced "pet er ing" (/ˈpœtərɪŋ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Lennart Poettering (systemd, PulseAudio). German.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "poh eh ter ing" /ˈpoʊətərɪŋ/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
