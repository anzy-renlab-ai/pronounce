How to pronounce buterin

person community-consensus

byoo ter in /ˈbjuːtərɪn/ mp3

buterin is most commonly pronounced "byoo ter in" (/ˈbjuːtərɪn/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Vitalik ("VEE-tah-leek") Buterin, co-founder of Ethereum.

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
