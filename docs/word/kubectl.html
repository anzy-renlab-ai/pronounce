How to pronounce kubectl

cli-tool contested

koob control /ˈkuːb kənˌtroʊl/ mp3
or: cube cuddle /ˈkjuːb kʌtəl/
or: kube C T L /ˌkjuːb siː tiː ˈɛl/

kubectl is most commonly pronounced "koob control" (/ˈkuːb kənˌtroʊl/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

K8s community readings vary widely. "koob-control" is heard from Kelsey Hightower and many maintainers; "cube-cuddle" is the running meme; some say it letter-by-letter.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "cube cuddle" /ˈkjuːb kʌtəl/
  • "kube C T L" /ˌkjuːb siː tiː ˈɛl/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will. Pronounce is a community-maintained dictionary — every entry tagged with a confidence level and (where possible) a citable source.

Hear it from the command line

$ say-it kubectl # primary × 3 + audible "or: …" for each alternate
$ say-it --alt kubectl # focus on the first alternate
$ say-it --all kubectl # primary AND every alternate, each repeated
$ say-it --solo kubectl # primary only, no "or: …" tail
$ say-it --why kubectl # print the dict entry with source URL
