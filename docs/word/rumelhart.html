How to pronounce rumelhart

person community-consensus

roo mul hart /ˈruːməlˌhɑːrt/ mp3

rumelhart is most commonly pronounced "roo mul hart" (/ˈruːməlˌhɑːrt/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

David Rumelhart (backprop, PDP); "ROO-mul-hart".

Source: Wikipedia
