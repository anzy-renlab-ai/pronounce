How to pronounce Kronecker

person community-consensus

kroh neck er /ˈkroʊnɛkər/ mp3
or: kron eck er /ˈkrɒnɛkər/

Kronecker is most commonly pronounced "kroh neck er" (/ˈkroʊnɛkər/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

German Leopold Kronecker, [ˈkʁoːnɛkɐ]; 'KROH-neck-er' with stress on first syllable, not 'kro-NECK-er'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "kron eck er" /ˈkrɒnɛkər/

Source: Wikipedia
