How to pronounce Debezium

project creator-clarified

Debezium is most commonly pronounced "dee bee zee um" (/diˈbiːziəm/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see Debezium official FAQ).

Official FAQ: "dee-BEE-zee-uhm" — say "DBs-ium" fast; from DBs + the periodic-table -ium suffix.

Source: Debezium official FAQ
