How to pronounce Schrodinger

person community-consensus

shroh ding er /ˈʃroʊdɪŋər/ mp3
or: shray ding er /ˈʃreɪdɪŋər/

Schrodinger is most commonly pronounced "shroh ding er" (/ˈʃroʊdɪŋər/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Austrian physicist Erwin Schrödinger (Schrödinger bridge); German 'SHROH-ding-er', not 'SHRAY-ding-er'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "shray ding er" /ˈʃreɪdɪŋər/

Source: Wikipedia
