How to pronounce GUI

acronym contested

gooey /ˈɡuːi/ mp3
or: G U I /ˌdʒiː juː ˈaɪ/
or: gwee /ɡwi/

GUI is most commonly pronounced "gooey" (/ˈɡuːi/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"GOOEY" is mainstream (matches Mandarin 故意 sound); "G-U-I" letter-by-letter is RMS/old-school; "gwee" is rare.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "G U I" /ˌdʒiː juː ˈaɪ/
  • "gwee" /ɡwi/

Source: Wikipedia

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
