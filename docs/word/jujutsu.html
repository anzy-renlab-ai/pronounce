How to pronounce jujutsu

cli-tool community-consensus

joo JUT soo /dʒuː ˈdʒʊt suː/ mp3

jujutsu is most commonly pronounced "joo JUT soo" (/dʒuː ˈdʒʊt suː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Git-compatible VCS (jj). Japanese "gentle art" — "joo-JUT-soo."

Source: jj VCS GitHub

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
