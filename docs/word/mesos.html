How to pronounce mesos

product contested

mee sose /ˈmiːsoʊs/ mp3
or: mess ose /ˈmɛsoʊs/

mesos is most commonly pronounced "mee sose" (/ˈmiːsoʊs/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Apache cluster manager; "MEE-sose" vs "MEH-sose".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "mess ose" /ˈmɛsoʊs/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
