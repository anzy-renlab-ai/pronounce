How to pronounce AUC

acronym community-consensus

ay you see /ˌeɪ juː ˈsiː/ mp3

AUC is most commonly pronounced "ay you see" (/ˌeɪ juː ˈsiː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Area Under the Curve (usually under ROC); letters "A-U-C".

Source: Wikipedia
