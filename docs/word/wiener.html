How to pronounce Wiener

person contested

wee ner /ˈwiːnər/ mp3
or: vee ner /ˈviːnər/

Wiener is most commonly pronounced "wee ner" (/ˈwiːnər/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Mathematician Norbert Wiener (Wiener process); English 'WEE-ner' is common, German-faithful 'VEE-ner' also used.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "vee ner" /ˈviːnər/

Source: Wikipedia § Talk:Norbert Wiener

How do you pronounce Wiener?

Wiener is pronounced "wee ner" (/ˈwiːnər/). Mathematician Norbert Wiener (Wiener process); English 'WEE-ner' is common, German-faithful 'VEE-ner' also used. Source: Wikipedia § Talk:Norbert Wiener.

What is the IPA for Wiener?

The IPA for Wiener is /ˈwiːnər/, respelled "wee ner".

Is Wiener sometimes pronounced "vee ner"?

Yes — "vee ner" (/ˈviːnər/) is one of the alternate readings of Wiener in active use.
