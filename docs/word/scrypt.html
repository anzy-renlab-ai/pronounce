How to pronounce scrypt

cs-term creator-clarified

S crypt /ɛs ˈkrɪpt/ mp3

scrypt is most commonly pronounced "S crypt" (/ɛs ˈkrɪpt/). This pronunciation is documented by the project itself (see Colin Percival (creator)).

Creator Colin Percival: "ess-crypt" (S then crypt). "script" is common but not the intent.

Source: Colin Percival (creator)
