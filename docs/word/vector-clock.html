How to pronounce vector clock

cs-term community-consensus

VEK ter clock /ˈvɛk tər klɒk/ mp3

vector clock is most commonly pronounced "VEK ter clock" (/ˈvɛk tər klɒk/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"VEK-ter clock". Logical-time data structure.

Source: Wikipedia
