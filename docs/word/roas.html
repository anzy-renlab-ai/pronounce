How to pronounce ROAS

acronym contested

ROW ass /ˈroʊ æs/ mp3
or: R O A S /ˌɑːr oʊ eɪ ˈɛs/

ROAS is most commonly pronounced "ROW ass" (/ˈroʊ æs/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"ROW-as" most common in performance marketing; some spell out. Return On Ad Spend.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "R O A S" /ˌɑːr oʊ eɪ ˈɛs/
