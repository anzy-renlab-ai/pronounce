How to pronounce zstd

abbreviation contested

zee standard /ˌziː ˈstændərd/ mp3
or: zee ess tee dee /ˌziː ɛs tiː ˈdiː/

zstd is most commonly pronounced "zee standard" (/ˌziː ˈstændərd/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Zstandard compression; "Z-standard" or spelled "Z-S-T-D".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "zee ess tee dee" /ˌziː ɛs tiː ˈdiː/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
