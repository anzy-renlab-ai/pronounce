How to pronounce Weibull

person contested

WHY bull /ˈwaɪbʊl/ mp3
or: VY bull /ˈveɪbʊl/

Weibull is most commonly pronounced "WHY bull" (/ˈwaɪbʊl/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

The Weibull distribution (reliability engineering, survival analysis, hazard/lifetime modeling, ML) is named after Swedish engineer Waloddi Weibull (1887–1979). General American speakers overwhelmingly say WHY-bull (/ˈwaɪbʊl/) with a true 'w' — this is the headword pronunciation in English Wikipedia, stress on the first syllable. But because Swedish 'W' is sounded as a 'V', the namesake-correct form is VY-bull (/ˈveɪbʊl/), which purists and many reliability engineers prefer; both are commonly heard in stats/ML talks. Either way the '-bull' is a reduced 'buul' (/-bʊl/), not 'bool'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "VY bull" /ˈveɪbʊl/

Source: Wikipedia § Weibull distribution (headword IPA /ˈwaɪbʊl/)
