How to pronounce difftastic

cli-tool community-consensus

diff TAS tik /dɪfˈtæs tɪk/ mp3

difftastic is most commonly pronounced "diff TAS tik" (/dɪfˈtæs tɪk/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"diff-TAS-tik". Structural diff.

Source: difftastic docs

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
