How to pronounce daemon

cs-term contested

dee mun /ˈdiːmən/ mp3
or: day mun /ˈdeɪmən/

daemon is most commonly pronounced "dee mun" (/ˈdiːmən/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Background process; classic "DEE-mun" vs "DAY-mun" split.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "day mun" /ˈdeɪmən/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
