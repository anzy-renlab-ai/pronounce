How to pronounce Log4Shell

cs-term community-consensus

log four shell /ˌlɒɡ fɔːr ˈʃɛl/ mp3

Log4Shell is most commonly pronounced "log four shell" (/ˌlɒɡ fɔːr ˈʃɛl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"log-four-shell". CVE-2021-44228. log4j RCE.
