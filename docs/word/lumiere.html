How do you pronounce Lumiere?

Lumiere is pronounced "loom yair" (/luːmˈjɛər/). Google's video diffusion model; French 'lumière' (light), 'loom-YAIR', stress on second syllable, not 'LOO-meer'. Source: Wiktionary.

What is the IPA for Lumiere?

The IPA for Lumiere is /luːmˈjɛər/, respelled "loom yair".
