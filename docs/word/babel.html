How to pronounce Babel

tool community-consensus

bab ul /ˈbæbəl/ mp3

Babel is most commonly pronounced "bab ul" (/ˈbæbəl/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"BAB-ul" — like the biblical tower.

Source: Babel
