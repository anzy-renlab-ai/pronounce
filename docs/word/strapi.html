How to pronounce strapi

product contested

strah pee /ˈstrɑːpi/ mp3
or: strap ee /ˈstræpi/

strapi is most commonly pronounced "strah pee" (/ˈstrɑːpi/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Headless CMS; "STRAH-pee" vs "STRAP-ee".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "strap ee" /ˈstræpi/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
