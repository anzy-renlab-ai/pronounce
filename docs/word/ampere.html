How to pronounce Ampere

product contested

AM peer /ˈæm pɪər/ mp3
or: AM pair /ˈæm pɛər/

Ampere is most commonly pronounced "AM peer" (/ˈæm pɪər/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

US: "AM-peer"; UK: "AM-pair". RTX 30-series + A100.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "AM pair" /ˈæm pɛər/

Source: NVIDIA Ampere
