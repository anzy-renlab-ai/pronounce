How to pronounce Radon

person contested

ray don /ˈreɪdɒn/ mp3
or: rah don /ˈrɑːdɒn/

Radon is most commonly pronounced "ray don" (/ˈreɪdɒn/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Austrian mathematician Johann Radon (Radon-Nikodym derivative); English 'RAY-don', German-faithful 'RAH-don' also heard.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "rah don" /ˈrɑːdɒn/

Source: Wikipedia

How do you pronounce Radon?

Radon is pronounced "ray don" (/ˈreɪdɒn/). Austrian mathematician Johann Radon (Radon-Nikodym derivative); English 'RAY-don', German-faithful 'RAH-don' also heard. Source: Wikipedia.

What is the IPA for Radon?

The IPA for Radon is /ˈreɪdɒn/, respelled "ray don".

Is Radon sometimes pronounced "rah don"?

Yes — "rah don" (/ˈrɑːdɒn/) is one of the alternate readings of Radon in active use.
