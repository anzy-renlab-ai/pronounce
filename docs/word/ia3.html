How to pronounce IA3

acronym community-consensus

eye ay three /ˌaɪ eɪ ˈθriː/ mp3

IA3 is most commonly pronounced "eye ay three" (/ˌaɪ eɪ ˈθriː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

(IA)³ — Infused Adapter by Inhibiting and Amplifying Inner Activations; letters + "three".

Source: Liu et al. 2022
