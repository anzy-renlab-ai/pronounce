How to pronounce DHCP

acronym community-consensus

DHCP is most commonly pronounced "D H C P" (/ˌdiː eɪtʃ siː ˈpiː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Letters "D-H-C-P".
