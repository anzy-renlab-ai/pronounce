How to pronounce t-SNE

acronym contested

tee ess en ee /ˌtiː ɛs ɛn ˈiː/ mp3
or: tee snee /ˈtiː ˌsniː/

t-SNE is most commonly pronounced "tee ess en ee" (/ˌtiː ɛs ɛn ˈiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

t-distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding; letters or "tee-snee".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "tee snee" /ˈtiː ˌsniː/

Source: Wikipedia

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will. Hear it from the command line

$ say-it --alt t-SNE # focus on the first alternate
$ say-it --all t-SNE # primary AND every alternate, each repeated
$ say-it --solo t-SNE # primary only, no "or: …" tail
$ say-it --why t-SNE # print the dict entry with source URL
