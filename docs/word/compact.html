How to pronounce compact

cs-term community-consensus

kum packt /kəmˈpækt/ mp3
or: kahm packt /ˈkɑːmpækt/

compact is most commonly pronounced "kum packt" (/kəmˈpækt/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

The verb (Claude Code /compact — compact the conversation) stresses the SECOND syllable: "kum-PAKT". The adjective/noun ("a compact format") stresses the first: "KAHM-pakt".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "kahm packt" /ˈkɑːmpækt/

Source: Merriam-Webster
