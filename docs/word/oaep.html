How to pronounce OAEP

acronym contested

O A E P /ˌoʊ eɪ iː ˈpiː/ mp3
or: WAY ep /ˈweɪ ɛp/

OAEP is most commonly pronounced "O A E P" (/ˌoʊ eɪ iː ˈpiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Letter-by-letter dominant; some crypto folks say "WAY-ep". Optimal Asymmetric Encryption Padding.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "WAY ep" /ˈweɪ ɛp/

Source: RFC 8017

How do you pronounce OAEP?

OAEP is pronounced "O A E P" (/ˌoʊ eɪ iː ˈpiː/). Letter-by-letter dominant; some crypto folks say "WAY-ep". Optimal Asymmetric Encryption Padding. Source: RFC 8017.

What is the IPA for OAEP?

The IPA for OAEP is /ˌoʊ eɪ iː ˈpiː/, respelled "O A E P".

Is OAEP sometimes pronounced "WAY ep"?

Yes — "WAY ep" (/ˈweɪ ɛp/) is one of the alternate readings of OAEP in active use.
