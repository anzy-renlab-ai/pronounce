How do you pronounce Grafana?

Grafana is pronounced "gra fah na" (/ɡrəˈfɑːnə/). "gra-FAH-na" is the most common community reading. Source: Grafana site.

What is the IPA for Grafana?

The IPA for Grafana is /ɡrəˈfɑːnə/, respelled "gra fah na".
