How to pronounce SOLID

acronym community-consensus

solid /ˈsɒlɪd/ mp3

SOLID is most commonly pronounced "solid" (/ˈsɒlɪd/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"SOL-id" — OOP design principles.

Source: Wikipedia
