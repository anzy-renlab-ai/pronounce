How to pronounce Majorana

product community-consensus

my uh rah nuh /ˌmaɪəˈrɑːnə/ mp3
or: mah yuh rah nuh /ˌmɑːjəˈrɑːnə/

Majorana is most commonly pronounced "my uh rah nuh" (/ˌmaɪəˈrɑːnə/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Microsoft's topological quantum chip line named after physicist Ettore Majorana; English usage is my-uh-RAH-nuh — the Italian j is a glide, never /dʒ/.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "mah yuh rah nuh" /ˌmɑːjəˈrɑːnə/

Source: Wikipedia — Ettore Majorana (/ˌmaɪ.əˈrɑːnə/, MY-ə-RAH-nə)
