How to pronounce Cypress

tool community-consensus

sigh press /ˈsaɪprəs/ mp3

Cypress is most commonly pronounced "sigh press" (/ˈsaɪprəs/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"SIGH-press" — like the tree.

Source: Cypress
