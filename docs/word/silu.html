How to pronounce SiLU

acronym contested

see loo /ˈsiːluː/ mp3
or: ess eye el yoo /ˌɛsaɪɛlˈjuː/

SiLU is most commonly pronounced "see loo" (/ˈsiːluː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Sigmoid Linear Unit activation (a.k.a. Swish); usually said as a word 'SEE-loo', sometimes spelled out 'S-I-L-U'.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ess eye el yoo" /ˌɛsaɪɛlˈjuː/

Source: Ultralytics Glossary
