How to pronounce altair

tool contested

al tair /ælˈtɛər/ mp3
or: al tire /ælˈtaɪər/

altair is most commonly pronounced "al tair" (/ælˈtɛər/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Python declarative viz library; "al-TAIR" or "al-TYRE", from the star.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "al tire" /ælˈtaɪər/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
