How to pronounce chroot

cli-tool contested

ch root /ˈtʃruːt/ mp3
or: change root /ˌtʃeɪndʒ ruːt/

chroot is most commonly pronounced "ch root" (/ˈtʃruːt/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"change root"; "ch-root" or said as "change-root".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "change root" /ˌtʃeɪndʒ ruːt/
