How to pronounce clippy

cli-tool community-consensus

klip ee /ˈklɪpi/ mp3

clippy is most commonly pronounced "klip ee" (/ˈklɪpi/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

Rust's official linter (run via `cargo clippy`). KLIP-ee — named after Clippy, the Microsoft Office paperclip assistant; same pronunciation as that mascot.

Source: rust-lang/rust-clippy GitHub

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
