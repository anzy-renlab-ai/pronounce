How to pronounce REPL

acronym contested

reh pəl /ˈrɛpəl/ mp3
or: R E P L /ˌɑːr iː piː ˈɛl/

REPL is most commonly pronounced "reh pəl" (/ˈrɛpəl/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"REP-uhl" common in Lisp/Clojure; letter-by-letter common elsewhere. Read-Eval-Print Loop.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "R E P L" /ˌɑːr iː piː ˈɛl/

Source: Wikipedia

How do you pronounce REPL?

REPL is pronounced "reh pəl" (/ˈrɛpəl/). "REP-uhl" common in Lisp/Clojure; letter-by-letter common elsewhere. Read-Eval-Print Loop. Source: Wikipedia.

What is the IPA for REPL?

The IPA for REPL is /ˈrɛpəl/, respelled "reh pəl".

Is REPL sometimes pronounced "R E P L"?

Yes — "R E P L" (/ˌɑːr iː piː ˈɛl/) is one of the alternate readings of REPL in active use.
