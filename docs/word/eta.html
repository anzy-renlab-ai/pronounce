How to pronounce eta

cs-term contested

ay tuh /ˈeɪtə/ mp3
or: ee tuh /ˈiːtə/

eta is most commonly pronounced "ay tuh" (/ˈeɪtə/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Greek letter η (learning rate); US math "AY-tuh", also "EE-tuh".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ee tuh" /ˈiːtə/
