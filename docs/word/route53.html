How to pronounce Route53

product contested

root fifty-three /ruːt ˌfɪf ti ˈθriː/ mp3
or: rowt fifty-three /raʊt ˌfɪf ti ˈθriː/

Route53 is most commonly pronounced "root fifty-three" (/ruːt ˌfɪf ti ˈθriː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

US tech "root"; UK / general "rowt". Port 53 = DNS.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "rowt fifty-three" /raʊt ˌfɪf ti ˈθriː/

Source: AWS Route 53

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls. Hearing the word a few times locks in the right reading better than reading IPA ever will.
