How to pronounce sac

abbreviation contested

sack /sæk/ mp3
or: ess ay see /ˌɛs eɪ ˈsiː/

sac is most commonly pronounced "sack" (/sæk/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Soft Actor-Critic; "sack" or letters "S-A-C".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ess ay see" /ˌɛs eɪ ˈsiː/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
