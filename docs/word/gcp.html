How to pronounce GCP

product community-consensus

G C P /ˌdʒiː siː ˈpiː/ mp3

GCP is most commonly pronounced "G C P" (/ˌdʒiː siː ˈpiː/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"G-C-P".

Source: Google Cloud
