How do you pronounce NiFi?

NiFi is pronounced "nigh fie" (/ˈnaɪˌfaɪ/). Apache dataflow tool from NSA's "NiagaraFiles"; the official FAQ states "nye fye" is the preferred pronunciation. Source: Apache NiFi FAQ (apache.org SVN).

What is the IPA for NiFi?

The IPA for NiFi is /ˈnaɪˌfaɪ/, respelled "nigh fie".
