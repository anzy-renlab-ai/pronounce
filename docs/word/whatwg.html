How to pronounce WHATWG

acronym contested

WAT wig /ˈwɒt wɪɡ/ mp3
or: W H A T W G /ˌdʌbəl juː eɪtʃ eɪ tiː ˌdʌbəl juː ˈdʒiː/

WHATWG is most commonly pronounced "WAT wig" (/ˈwɒt wɪɡ/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

"WHAT-wig" common; letter-by-letter also heard. Web Hypertext Application Technology Working Group.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "W H A T W G" /ˌdʌbəl juː eɪtʃ eɪ tiː ˌdʌbəl juː ˈdʒiː/

Source: WHATWG
