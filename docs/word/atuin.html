How to pronounce atuin

cli-tool contested

AY too in /ˈeɪ tuː ɪn/ mp3
or: uh TOO in /ə ˈtuː ɪn/

atuin is most commonly pronounced "AY too in" (/ˈeɪ tuː ɪn/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Shell history manager. Aztec turtle deity — "AY-too-in" common.

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "uh TOO in" /ə ˈtuː ɪn/

Source: Atuin
