How to pronounce semaphore

cs-term community-consensus

sem a for /ˈsɛməˌfɔːr/ mp3

semaphore is most commonly pronounced "sem a for" (/ˈsɛməˌfɔːr/). This is the widely-used reading among engineers, though edge cases exist.

"SEM-uh-for".

Source: Wikipedia
