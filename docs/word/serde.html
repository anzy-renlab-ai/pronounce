How to pronounce serde

tool contested

sir dee /sɜːrˈdiː/ mp3
or: ser dee /ˈsɜːrdi/

serde is most commonly pronounced "sir dee" (/sɜːrˈdiː/). This is one of several readings in active use; the developer community hasn't converged on a single answer.

Rust serialization framework; "sir-DEE" (serialize/deserialize), some say "SER-dee".

Alternate readings you might hear:

  • "ser dee" /ˈsɜːrdi/

Pronouncing project and product names correctly avoids the small but persistent friction of being gently corrected during standups, conference Q&As, and team calls.
